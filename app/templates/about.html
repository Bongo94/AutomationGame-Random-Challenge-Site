About This Project

This Automation Challenge Generator was developed by Bongo94 (you can find me on GitHub) to enhance the gameplay experience for Automation: The Car Company Tycoon Game.

The goal of this tool is to help players generate unique and structured design briefs, either from pre-defined templates or by using a highly customizable rule-based system. It aims to add an extra layer of creativity and challenge to your car designs within the game.

What is Automation: The Car Company Tycoon Game?

Automation is a car company tycoon game developed by Camshaft Software. In Automation, you design and build cars from scratch, managing everything from engine design and chassis engineering to production lines and marketing. It's known for its incredibly detailed and realistic car design mechanics.

Features Highlight:

  • Generate challenges based on pre-defined templates.
  • Create custom rules for various car categories.
  • Support for multiple players.
  • Dynamic UI with AJAX for quick rerolls.
  • Save custom configurations as new templates.
